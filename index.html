How to Edit a Photo

Assembly time: 45 minutes
Awesome rating: 120% awesome

Editing photos is a highly valuable skills in the world of journalism and I will teach you the basics step by step.

  • Things You'll Need

  • A smart device
  • A photo
  • Photo editing software
  • The internet

  1. Open the editing software on a device of your choice.

    This is what we're going to use to edit whatever we need to in the photo.

  2. Insert the the photo into the software.

    This can be done by either dragging it from your files or using the insert or add tab.

    Photo of woman.

  3. Add any effects or color you want to your photo to make it how you like.

    Keep in mind to try and make the photo as clear as possible.

  4. Check your work.

    Make sure that everything that you wanted done to the photo is how you like it.

  5. Export the photo to your device or storage.

    Make sure to export it in the format that best suits your purpose.
